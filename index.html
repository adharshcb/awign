Gig Work Platform

Businesses get their work done. Awign upskills people and provides right tools, driving them towards work fulfillment.


We are the largest enterprise gig work platform where we take up on-the-ground or digital recurring work from enterprises and get it done through a million gig workers across 500 cities in India.

Enterprises give us their requirements. We convert them into simpler tasks - full time work, part time work, internships, and make them live on our platform.

Gig workers apply on the available tasks, get selected, get trained and then go out to work in their city to complete the tasks in the Awign app.

Gig workers get paid on completion of tasks and Awign bills the enterprises for completed tasks post thorough quality control measures.

Workforce struggle with lack of industry skills and relevant jobs in their cities. Few are ambitious, some want to earn their bread & butter and others want to learn & grow. Awign drives them towards these aspirations by providing full time jobs, part time jobs & internships and becomes an execution partner in their entire job cycle.

A growing Enterprise takes up huge risk and investment while employing and managing thousands of full time workers across varied geographies. With no guarantee of results and no proper way to monitor the ground reality, it is an uphill task. Awign's outcome-driven no code tech leverages the gig economy to deliver core Enterprise work at scale across varied business functions.








Awign works with enterprises to take up intricate work across the service lines of auditing, diligence, assessments, proctoring, last-mile delivery, new business development, and digital gigs, which have to be done in distributed locations in Pan India through gig workers.
